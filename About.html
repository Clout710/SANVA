ABOUT US

SANVA is a non-profit organization that administers vaccines in the privacy of your own home. We want to reach out to those who don't have access to medical facilities (such as clinics, community centers, and hospitals), as well as those who are anti-vaccine. We want to teach them why the vaccination is beneficial to them and to all of us. People who are sluggish and cannot or will not wait in necessary lines. Our team of qualified experts will advise you on the different types of vaccinations available in your area, as well as how they will benefit you and your family in the future. Our goal is to make our country a better place to live for today's citizens and future generations. As a result, we can live a normal life.

OUR HISTORY

The company was founded in September 2021 and is headquartered in Woodstock, Cape Town. In September 2021, we formed a group of well-versed Systems Developers, all of whom have a growth mindset, to try to figure out what difficulties we have based on today's society. They arrived with a clever method of delivering vaccine to your home.

BRAND MOTTO

To improve our health status through the prevention of the Covid-19 disease, and to consistently improve the healthcare delivery system by focusing on access, equity, efficiency, quality and sustainability.
