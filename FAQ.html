Frequently Asked Questions

What is SANVA?

SANVA (South African National Vaccination Assonation) is a non-provited orginisation that provides the vaccine injection(s),straight from the comfort of your home

Are we legit?

YES WE ARE!! We are a non-provited company ,supported by the government.

Is the COVID-19 vaccine safe?

Vaccines were authorized or approved by the Food and Drug Administration (FDA) have been thoroughly tested and found to be safe and effective in preventing severe COVID-19. They continue to undergo continuous and intense safety monitoring.
For more about the safety of the vaccine go to www.hopkinsmedicine.org

What is Covid-19?

Coronavirus disease 2019 (COVID-19) ,also known as COVID and the coronavirus ,is a contagious disease caused by severe acute respiratory syndrome coronavirus 2 (SARS-CoV-2). The first known case was identified in Wuhan ,China ,in December 2019. The disease has since spread worldwide ,leading to an ongoing pandemic.

What we provide?

We aim for those people with does not have access to any medical centres (thus includes Clinics ,Community centres ,Hospitals,etc)

How it works?

First you have to register on the registration section. Afterwards you will receive a sms on your phone to tell you when we will be in your neighborhood . Our trained nurses will assist you in your house & after injection they will wait for a period of 15-20 minutes just to ensure no possible side-effects does not occur.

Our goal is to make our Nation a better place to live for the people of today & for our future generations to come. so we can live normal again amongst everyone.
